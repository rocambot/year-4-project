General Approach in Design

The concept of responsive and simplistic design are always applied for this project.

instead off putting a lot of content into one specific part, spliting off content and placing them into different position would be a good option.

Visually focused is applied in this project. Therefore, more photographs or visual arts are applied in the project.

Planning

I have looked over a lot of celebrities and merchandise sites in the planning stage of this project before I actually develop the site.

For example,I took reference from the official site of a Cantopop artist- Hins Cheung.https://hinscheung.com/tc/home. The inspiration of the video mainpage is actually comes from his official website.

Most of the other part of the site, I took references from the Gucci website. For example, The site always have a hero-image that on the top of the page in their women, men, or other collection pages.

The remaining rest of them took references from W3schools and from the class. For instance, the navigation bar, footer navigation and the social links in the footer section.

Development & Content Production

In the content production process, most of the photographs and graphics arts comes from my own work. Additionally, the font comes from Google font-https://fonts.google.com/

For example, the video used different angle to shoot it. It used the tilt, truck, dolly shots to shoot the video.

The reason of why some icons like Microsoft office icon and Adobe Creative Cloud are chosen from the cdn or from the internet search is because they are much easier to identify and interpret those logos, since everyone know the icon.

The social media icon in the footer section comes from the CDN-https://cdnjs.com/

In the development process, most of the website were developed successfully under my plan. But of course, there were some modifications for some of the pages.

For instance, the webpage of "Details of Works", I originally wanted to make all my projects put into one single page, and I thought smooth scrolling would work in the another page. However, it can't. Therefore, what I did was I open three new HTML page for each project.

The another thing was the responsive hero-image. To make it responsive, there were a lot of coding that put into the media query in order to make it work.
