CCT460 Project- My Products

The CCT460 project used the CMS Wordpress to work on the project. The remarkable thing in this project for me was the commerce system in Workpress. Instead of need to create a complex coding system. Only a plug-in "Woocommerce" install in WordPress, then, everything is ready to go. Only users have to customize information by themselves.

Details of My Products

This is one of the detail of my products after I clicked the thumbnail of the photo. Users can see the description of the products and leave a feedback if they like. As the users like the product, they can purchase it by clicking the button "Add to cart".

Cart

This page allow users to see what kind of products they want to pruchase. As they can add or delete the product they want to buy. If they want to purchase the product, they can click on the "proceed to payment" button to proceed the bill information.

Bill information and Payment page

This is the bill information and payment page where users can fill their bill information and their payment approaches.
